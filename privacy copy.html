  • Identify Usage Trends: To better understand how our Services are being used so we can improve them.
  • Request Feedback: To contact you and get your feedback about your use of the Services.
  • User-to-User Communication: To allow for communications with another user of our Services if you choose.

  • Compliance with Legal Obligations: To comply with our legal and contractual obligations and to protect our legal interests.
