How Does

SwiftGuard Responder Africa Work?

Step #1: Alarm Triggered

Monitoring companies are notified when an alarm is activated at a location.

Step #2: Alarm Dispatched

Licensed guards and personal protectors receive the alarm alert instantly.

Step #3: Responder Accepts Alarm

The nearest responder accepts the alarm, with progress tracked in real-time.

Step #4: Incident Report Submitted

The guard submits a detailed report through the app, including photos and timestamps.

Step #5: Site Secured

Within minutes, the site is secured, and peace of mind is restored.

Step #6: Continuous Monitoring

SwiftGuard provides continuous monitoring and updates, ensuring ongoing safety and quick responses to any future alarms.
